## Community Call

We have a community call every 2 weeks. Alternating between two times.

* 11 am \(UTC + 8\)
* 5 pm \(UTC + 8\)

Please join the [**feast-dev**](getting-help.md#feast-development) mailing list to receive the the calendar invitation.
